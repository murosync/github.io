About MuroSync

MuroSync is a high-precision timing and synchronization platform engineered for distributed scientific and industrial systems. It delivers deterministic sub-nanosecond timing over optical links, enabling reliable synchronization across multiple remote nodes.

Key Features

  • Sub-nanosecond timing resolution
  • Automatic delay calibration and phase alignment better than 100 ps
  • Optical timing distribution over fiber for long-distance, low-jitter operation
  • FPGA-based modular architecture
  • Low-latency event and trigger distribution
