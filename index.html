MuroSynch

Sub-nanosecond timing & synchronization hardware for large-scale scientific facilities.

MuroSynch is an FPGA-based timing module built on Artix™ UltraScale+ that distributes clocks and trigger events with sub-nanosecond precision over optical links. It targets accelerator complexes, synchrotron light sources, FELs and other large-scale experimental facilities.

  • Sub-ns timing jitter with optical link delay compensation
  • 4–8 SFP/SFP+ ports for distributed timing networks
  • Programmable SMA trigger outputs with per-channel delay
  • Conceptually compatible with MRF / White Rabbit style timing systems via a custom protocol
